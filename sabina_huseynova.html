Our Team

Sabina Huseynova
Member of the Board

Second-year student at Vrije Universiteit Brussels (VUB) and Gent University double program in Politics and Social Sciences. Proficient in research techniques, data management and has sound knowledge of economics with a focus on macroeconomic policy, social policy, corporate governance, and financial policy. Has a comprehensive and systematic overview of political processes both in EU and South Caucasus region. After graduating from her first Bachelor degree in Translation and Culture Studies, she has been working for three years at the diplomatic missions of Spain and Argentina in Azerbaijan where she was involved in activities of administrative, political and cultural sections of the diplomatic representations. She speaks fluently Russian, English, Spanish, Catalan, Turkish and French languages.

Bachelor's degree Politics and Social Sciences
