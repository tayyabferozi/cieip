Our Team

Javida Rahimova
Member of the Board | VUB Univ. COORD

Javida has been an intern at the Ministry of Culture and Tourism of the Republic of Azerbaijan, the Establishment and Regulation of Communications in the International Cooperation Department. In 2019, she had a work practice with the deputies of the Republic of Azerbaijan on the writing and regulating of 'International Agreements' documents during her internship at the Milli Majlis of the Azerbaijan Republic. Besides that, Javida has been a personal trainer, organizing and regulating meetings and events, at the “WOWOMAN Empowers Azerbaijan”. As of her specialization, she has participated in many MUN Simulation games. Between the years 2017-2020, she has taken active participation in more than 10 of these competitions, particularly the “2019 III MILLIMUN” – National MUN and 2017 National OIC simulations held by Models of United Nations and Organization of Islamic Cooperation. Javida has also organized many intellectual games at her educational institutions, such as “Brain Ring” and “Xemse”.

“Master of Political Science: European and International Governance” program, Vrije Universiteit Brussel (VUB)
