Tackling landmines in the EU neighbourhood

On June 21, we were pleased to take part in the conference "Tackling landmines in the EU neighbourhood: Evolving threats and the need for enhanced cooperation" in the European Parliament.

Our organisation was represented by our member Javida Rahimova

During the event on landmines in EU neighbourhoods (Azerbaijan, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Croatia), speakers addressed many challenges on the ground and discussed prospects for cooperation with the EU.
