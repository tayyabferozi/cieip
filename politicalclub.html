Political Club

The Political Club of the Caspian Initiative for European Integration and Partnership (CIEIP) aims to follow political developments in the Caspian region, European Neighborhood, and the European Union’s response to particular events concerning the mentioned regions. For this purpose, the Club has ten active members who analyze a wide range of political affairs, such as economic & business policies, environmental policies, energy, society, and foreign policy. Each CIEIP member can join the Political Club of the organization. Our activity mainly involves conducting independent research, publishing publications, organizing events, discussions, forums, and panels, participating in related events, building connections with influencers, policy-makers, diplomats, think tanks, media organizations, NGOs, student & youth organizations, and state institutions. So far, the members of the Political Club have published a couple of articles in Parliament Magazine and EU Political Report. We organized several events in the context of peacebuilding between Azerbaijan and Armenia with the participation of Members of the European Parliament, think tank staff, students, and other youth organizations. It is our pleasure to note that the Club members have been invited to a number of events in the European Parliament, and we have actively taken part in many think tanks’ events.
