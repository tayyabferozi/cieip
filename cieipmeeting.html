Azerbaijani Cultural Day in Brussels

Tuesday 29 April

CIEIP Meeting

On the 25th of January, Caspian Initiative for European Integration and Partnership (CIEIP) held an online event for the winners who were not able to join our event in Brussels Press Club. The meeting aimed to have online discussions and enhance the existing networks of our organization. Winners from various countries have been invited and awarded prizes for their active participation in November's discussion forum about the Second Karabakh War and Peace Perspectives.

Meeting

Two participants were the winners of the peace proposal competition, who won €200 for writing about peace perspectives between Azerbaijan and Armenia while three winners won €50 for successfully completing a quiz about the Karabakh conflict. One of our participants was successful both in the quiz and peace proposal competition. During the event, we agreed to strengthen the ties and collaborate in the future. We congratulate our winners and wish them good luck!
